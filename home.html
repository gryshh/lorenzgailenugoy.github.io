Traditional

Filipinos have a rich culinary tradition of enjoying kakanin, a variety of rice-based delicacies often served during special occasions and gatherings. These sweet and sticky treats, such as bibingka, puto, and sapin-sapin, are deeply rooted in Filipino culture and heritage.
